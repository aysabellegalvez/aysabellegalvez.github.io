Artie Vierkant

Work Explanation

Image Object is “a body of sculptural works that existed between the physical object and the mediated digital image, and through their existence highlighted the increasingly fluid boundary between the two.”

Brings up the conversation of

The Impact of Digital Technologies on Physical Art

“Each work in the Image Objects series originates as a digital file, which is then printed on aluminum composite panels and precision cut so that they appear three-dimensional.”

Photos are taken of the physical works, which are then again generated into new tactical objects, therefore making derivatives of each piece.

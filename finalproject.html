Celestial Crash Course

Astrology is the prediction of every day events and human personalities based on locations of celestial bodies: the planets and stars.

The existing motions and configurations between all these bodies are believed to affect the relationships among predicted events.

This website was brought to you to create a simplied and interactive version of everything astrology. Mostly highlighting the more popular aspect of astrology: relatable character traits.
